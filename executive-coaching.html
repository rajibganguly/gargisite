Executive Coaching

Anamitra Chatterjee, Accredited Executive Coach (EMCC) holds a Master’s degree, MSc Masters in Executive
Coaching (AMEC) from Hult Ashridge Centre for Coaching (ACC), UK.

Executive Coaching delivers value. “Value is valued by those who value value.” – Rabindranath Tagore

Anamitra’s approach to coaching

“My leadership development and executive coaching practice is informed by own belief in
Performance = Potential – (Internal) Interference. I I rely on this belief and the relational coaching approach to
support my clients to help them explore behavioural change and attain their goals. I use my training,
accreditation and experience to connect with leaders on the warmth-competency bridge.”

“I believe that connection, rapport and trust between coach and client leads to a co-created relationship.”

This relational methodology is at the core of my approach for effective executive coaching engagements.
I encourage leaders to develop their own personal leadership style informed by their behavioural drivers and
preferences, for deeper impact.

I offer tailored executive coaching (EC) experiences for my clients that meets the needs of individual leaders
and organizations. I customize the coaching with the clients’ choice of face-to-face and online sessions, the
frequency and duration of EC sessions are agreed with the client. I do offer flexibility within an agreed range.

An individual face-to-face executive coaching (EC) assignment for Directors and CXOs (of similiar seniority,
education and work experience) is tailored for the development needs of the individual. A typical EC
engagement is covered in 8-10 EC sessions, of 60-90 mins each including an impactful mindful hour! EC entails
about 12 hours of individual executive coaching conversations, with psychometrics, reflection journals and
development assignments dovetailed between the EC sessions over a 4-5 month period.

I also offer executive coaching through an online digital coaching platform (coaching.com).

Anamitra has 11 years of Leadership Development & Executive Coaching practice. His skills of listening, inquiry,
exploration, challenge and support are fundamental to development of leaders and organizational
performance. Anamitra specialises in supporting his executive coaching clients to develop coaching skills
from his skills of listening, inquiry, exploration, challenge and support

He has coached over 220 senior leaders and 2500 hours. He is a certified practitioner of psychometrics:
Hogan & LVI360, Belbin, FIRO-B & MBTI.
