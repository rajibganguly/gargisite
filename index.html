About Anamitra

Anamitra Chatterjee, Accredited Executive Coach has had education and experience of
25+ years in India & UK across Deutsche Bank and PwC, Hult Ashridge UK & IIMs in India.

Anamitra Chatterjee has had diverse education and work experience of 25+ years in India & UK across Deutsche
Bank and PwC, Hult Ashridge UK and IIMs in India. He is an Accredited Executive Coach (EMCC).
He has 11 years of Leadership Development & Executive Coaching practice. He has coached over 220 senior leaders
and 2500 hours. As Faculty, he designs and delivers executive education programmes on consulting skills, leadership
and team development. As Facilitator, he has delivered training to over 3000 managers.
